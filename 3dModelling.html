MentorMe - AI Powered Career Guidance
Objectives
Firstly, to seamlessly integrate artificial intelligence connecting individuals, fostering meaningful conversations between students and mentors. This dynamic interaction mutually benefits both parties. Secondly, the app tackles a pervasive issue by empowering users to chart a career path based on their current position, existing skills, and future aspirations to a high level of specificity. MentorMe thus serves as a solution to help users navigate their professional progression effectively.
The job market is undergoing a seismic shift with the rising influence of artificial intelligence. Anticipating this transformation within the next few years and motivated by the dual challenges of economic downturn and the need for retraining, this app was developed to address the evolving demands of the workplace.
